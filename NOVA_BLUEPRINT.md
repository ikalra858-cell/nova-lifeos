# 🌌 NOVA Blueprint

**Version:** 1.0

**Project:** NOVA - Adaptive AI Life Operating System

**Founder:** Isha Kalra

---

# Vision

NOVA is an adaptive AI Life Operating System that learns with the user's permission, remembers what matters, and proactively helps people achieve their goals.

Unlike traditional AI assistants that simply answer questions, NOVA builds a long-term understanding of the user's preferences, habits, goals, and workflows to provide meaningful, personalized assistance.

NOVA is designed to become a trusted companion that helps people organize their digital lives while encouraging continuous growth.

---

# Mission

To create an AI companion that understands people before trying to optimize them.

NOVA exists to reduce the mental burden of managing modern digital life by connecting information, remembering important context, and providing proactive support without taking control away from the user.

---
